Questions About Listing Your Agency

How do I get listed?

If your agency already appears in our directory, simply claim it using the form above. If not, fill out the claim form with your details and we will create your listing within 48 hours.

Can I try a paid plan before committing?

The Free tier gives you a permanent listing at no cost. Upgrade to a paid plan anytime and cancel within 14 days for a full refund if you are not satisfied.

What are referral leads?

When a tenant views your contact details, clicks through to your website, or requests more information, that counts as a referral lead. Each plan includes a monthly allocation of leads.

Can I respond to reviews?

Yes, on Starter and above plans. Responding to reviews shows prospective tenants that you are engaged and care about feedback. Pro members get priority display for their responses.
