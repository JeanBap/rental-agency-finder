Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rental Agency Finder?

Rental Agency Finder is the first European-wide directory that helps renters find, compare, and review rental agencies. Unlike property listing sites, we focus on the agencies themselves, helping you find trustworthy partners for your rental search.

Why do I need to submit a review to see contact details?

Our review-gating system ensures we build a genuine, helpful database of agency reviews. By sharing your experience with one agency, you help other renters make better decisions, and in return you get full access to contact details across all listed agencies for 30 days.

Is it free for renters?

Yes, completely free. Browse agencies, read reviews, and access contact details after submitting one review. No sign-up fees, no hidden costs.

How do you verify agencies?

We cross-reference agency data with local business registries, check their online presence, and verify through Google Maps listings. Agencies on paid tiers undergo additional verification. Reviews from verified tenants are marked accordingly.

What countries do you cover?

We cover 36 European countries including Spain, Portugal, Italy, Greece, France, Germany, Netherlands, UK, and more. We are continuously expanding our city coverage within each country.
