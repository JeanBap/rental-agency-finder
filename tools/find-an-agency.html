How This Tool Works

This decision tree considers five factors: your rental duration, budget sensitivity, language needs, whether you are relocating internationally, and what type of property you need. Based on your combination of answers, it recommends one of eight agency profiles and links you directly to filtered results in our directory.

The recommendations are based on our agency scoring methodology, which evaluates agencies across seven criteria including response time, fee transparency, and language support. For a deeper understanding of costs, try our rental cost calculator.
