Rental Contract Clause Checklists

Bilingual reference for every clause in a European lease. Select your language to see local terms alongside English translations.

How to Use This Checklist

When you receive a rental contract in a foreign language, open this checklist alongside the document. Match each local-language term to its English equivalent and check whether the clause is present, missing, or different from what you expected. Clauses marked Required are legally mandated in most contracts for that country. Clauses marked Watch are where disputes most commonly arise.

This is a reference tool, not legal advice. For a deeper explanation of each clause type, read our rental contracts explained guide. To understand your deposit rights, see our deposit law comparison table.

Need Help Understanding Your Contract?

A good rental agency will walk you through every clause and provide a translation. Find one that speaks your language.
