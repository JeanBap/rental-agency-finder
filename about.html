About Rental Agency Finder

Europe's first directory for finding, comparing, and reviewing rental agencies

Our Mission

Rental Agency Finder is Europe's first comprehensive directory dedicated to helping renters find, compare, and review rental agencies. We believe that every tenant deserves access to trustworthy, vetted rental agencies—regardless of which European city they're moving to.

Our mission is simple: empower renters with transparent, community-driven reviews so they can make informed decisions about which agency to trust with their housing.

The Problem We Solve

Millions of people relocate, travel, or work remotely across Europe every year. Whether you're looking for a short-term apartment in Barcelona, a mid-term flat in Berlin, or a long-term rental in Athens—you need to work with a local rental agency.

But finding a good agency is frustrating. Property listing sites like Airbnb and Booking.com show you properties, but tell you nothing about the agencies behind them. You're left searching scattered Google reviews, asking friends, and hoping for the best.

Questions like "Which agency responds quickly?" "Which one has fair contracts?" and "Which one actually maintains properties?" remain unanswered until you've already signed a lease.

How It Works

For Renters

  1. Search for agencies by country, city, and rental type (short-term, mid-term, long-term).
  2. Read reviews from real tenants who have rented through each agency. See ratings on responsiveness, fairness, and property quality.
  3. Get in touch with the agencies you're interested in. After submitting your own review, you unlock contact details for all agencies for 30 days.

Why the review requirement? It ensures our database grows with genuine, helpful reviews from real renters—not spam or fake feedback.

For Agencies

  1. Every agency gets a free listing in our directory.
  2. Agencies can claim their profile, respond to reviews, and build their reputation.
  3. Upgrade to paid plans for featured placement, verified badges, and qualified tenant referrals.

Pricing: Starter (EUR 29/mo), Advanced (EUR 79/mo), Pro (EUR 199/mo)

Our Team

Rental Agency Finder was founded in 2026 by Yanni Papoutsis, a passionate advocate for transparent rental markets and tenant rights across Europe.

The platform was built to solve a real problem Yanni experienced firsthand: the difficulty of finding trustworthy rental agencies when moving to a new city. What started as a personal frustration has grown into a comprehensive directory helping thousands of renters across Europe.
