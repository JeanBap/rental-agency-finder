About Rental Agency Finder

Europe's first directory dedicated to rental agencies

Rental Agency Finder was born from a simple frustration: when you need to rent a property in a European city you do not know, how do you find a trustworthy rental agency?

Property listing sites like Airbnb, Booking.com, and Flatio show you properties, but they tell you nothing about the agencies behind them. Which agency responds quickly? Which one has fair contracts? Which one actually maintains the properties they manage?

The Problem We Solve

Millions of people relocate, travel, or work remotely across Europe every year. They need short-term, mid-term, or long-term rentals, and most end up working with a local rental agency. But finding a good agency means hours of searching, reading scattered Google reviews, and hoping for the best.

Rental Agency Finder changes that. We are building the first comprehensive, review-driven directory of rental agencies across 36 European countries and 60+ cities. Tenants can search by location and rental type, read verified reviews from real tenants, and make informed decisions about which agency to trust with their housing.

How It Works for Tenants

Search for agencies in your target city. Read reviews from tenants who have rented through each agency. To access full contact details, submit a review of an agency you have used yourself. This review-gating model ensures our database grows organically with genuine, helpful reviews.

How It Works for Agencies

Every agency gets a free listing in our directory. Agencies can claim their profile, respond to reviews, and upgrade to paid tiers for featured placement, verified badges, and qualified tenant referrals. Our goal is to connect good agencies with good tenants.

Our Coverage

We cover 36 European countries from Spain and Portugal to Scandinavia and the Balkans. Within each country, we focus on the cities with the strongest rental markets, including Barcelona, Lisbon, Rome, Athens, Paris, Berlin, Amsterdam, Prague, Budapest, Vienna, London, Dublin, Copenhagen, Stockholm, and many more.
