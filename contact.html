Contact FAQ

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to hear back?

We aim to respond to all emails within 24–48 hours. During peak times (weekends, holidays), it may take up to 72 hours. We prioritize agency inquiries and bug reports.

Can I call instead of emailing?

We operate email-only support to ensure detailed, documented responses. Email is the best way to reach us, and it creates a helpful record for both parties.

How do I request a feature or suggest an improvement?

Email us your feature request at info@rentalagencyfinder.com. Be specific about what you'd like to see and why it would help. We review all suggestions and implement the most requested features.

What if I found a bug or security issue?

Please report it immediately to info@rentalagencyfinder.com with as much detail as possible (steps to reproduce, screenshots, affected browser/device). For security issues, mark your email as urgent and include "SECURITY" in the subject line.

I submitted a review or inquiry but haven't heard back. What should I do?

Check your spam folder first. If you still don't see a response, reply to your original email or send a new one with "FOLLOW-UP" in the subject. We may have missed it, and we'll help immediately.

Where is Rental Agency Finder based?

We're based in Greece, part of the European rental market we serve. Our team works across multiple time zones to support renters and agencies across Europe.

Do you have an office address or mailing address?

We operate digitally and email-only. You can reach us at info@rentalagencyfinder.com. For official inquiries, we can provide additional details via email.

Can I request to have my agency removed from the listing?

Yes. Email us with your agency name and location, and we'll remove it within 24 hours. This is a permanent action, so please ensure it's what you want.
